Get the CellValue contents as a T.

pub fn convert(&self, cell_type: CellType) -> Result<Self>

Convert self into a variant with CellType cell_type equal to or wider than its current CellType.

Returns Ok(CellValue) if the CellType of cell_type is the same or wider than the encoded value, or Err(Error) if T is narrower.

source

pub fn unify(&self, other: &Self) -> (Self, Self)

Determines the smallest cell type that can contain self and other, and then converts values to that cell type and returns a tuple of the converted values, i.e. (convert(self), convert(other)).
